Contained here are dynamic gas exchange data used in the PhD thesis of Ross M. Deans, entitled: 'The economic challenge of leaf gas exchange'.

Excel files showing individual replicates for each species for an increase in irradiance from 50-1000 umol photons m-2 s-1 PAR are shown in 'light50-1000'.

Excel files showing individual replicates for each species for a decrease in irradiance from 1000-50 umol photons m-2 s-1 PAR are shown in 'light1000-50'.

The Excel file showing individual replicates for each species for dark respiration is shown in 'photo'.

Each file contains data in columns for time (s), assimilation rate (A, umol m-2 s-1), transpiration rate (E, mmol m-2 s-1), stomatal conductance to water vapour (g, mol m-2 s-1), internal concentration of CO2 (Ci, umol mol-1), ambient concentration of CO2 (Ca, umol mol-1), leaf-to-air vapour pressure difference (VPDL, kPa), respectively.

Species name is specified in the first two letters of the filename:
Ac = Austrocedrus chilensis
Ad = Adiantum capillus-veneris
Cp = Callitris preissii
Da = Dicksonia antarctica
Ep = Eucalyptus pauciflora
Gh = Gossypium hirsutum
Hi = Histiolepis incisa
Hr = Hypolepis rugolusa
Md = Marsilea drummondii
Nc = Nothofagus cunninghamii
Pl = Prumnopitys ladei
Pr = Pinus radiata
So = Spinacea oleracea
Vf = Vicia faba
Vv = Vitis vinifera
